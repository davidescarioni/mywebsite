Beware The Frog

Made by Davide Scarioni (@5c4r10) for Game Maker Italia 2022 Competition

Beware the Frog is a platform game where you (a frog) can jump, run, walljump and... use your tongue to reach the walls! And to eat enemies, of course!

Beware the Frog has 19+1 levels where you need to reach the exit dodging enemies and collecting coins. Or.. you can just run to the exit in the speedrun mode!

Play it on Opera GX.games
